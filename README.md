# Shield Insurance Insight
### Insurance Business Intelligence & Performance Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview

**Shield Insurance Insight** is an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard developed in **Power BI** to analyze insurance business performance and support data-driven decision-making.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of customer acquisition, revenue performance, policy trends, sales channel effectiveness, and demographic analysis through interactive visualizations.

> **Project Duration:** November 2022 – April 2023 (Sample Business Data)

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project is to help business stakeholders monitor key performance indicators, identify growth opportunities, analyze customer behavior, and evaluate sales performance using a centralized analytics dashboard.

## 📈 Key Business Insights

- Customers aged **31–50 years** represent the largest customer segment and contribute the highest policy purchases.
- **Offline Agent** is the highest-performing sales channel, generating the largest share of customers and revenue.
- Digital channels such as **Online App** and **Website** are showing consistent growth, indicating increasing customer preference for digital services.
- The **18–30** age group has comparatively lower policy adoption, presenting an opportunity for targeted marketing initiatives.
- Revenue and customer acquisition demonstrate steady growth during the analysis period.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Strengthen the Offline Agent network through training and performance incentives.
- Increase investment in digital marketing to accelerate online policy sales.
- Develop personalized insurance products for the 31–50 age segment.
- Introduce affordable, digital-first insurance plans targeting younger customers.
- Monitor monthly policy performance to identify emerging business trends.
